Mission Statement:

Size8.org, Inc., a scientific research organization and an innovative nonprofit that focused on applied research to solve recognized needs. Offers supervised structured and guided work-based training to improve an executive trainee's capabilities and knowledge. To develop and improve communities through the effective action and leadership of trained individual. It is primarily organized for scientific, educational and charitable purposes.
